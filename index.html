Single Player Two Player

Aeronom is a fast-paced retro-style action-simulation game where high-tech drones meet the gritty world of industrial emissions. Step into the role of an airborne monitor pilot, guiding your nimble quadrotor through sprawling refineries and towering offshore platforms. Your mission: track invisible emission plumes, dodge scorching flares, and collect vital data that could save the planet.

Every sortie is a battle between precision and chaos. Gusts of wind shift toxic clouds, flares ignite with unpredictable fury, and your drone’s delicate sensors demand steady hands. Quick reflexes keep you alive, but sharp strategy keeps your score climbing. Will you play it safe, or risk venturing deeper into the plume for maximum readings?

Featuring vibrant pixel-art backdrops, pulsing electronic music, and comic-style sprites, Aeronom delivers both old-school charm and a modern environmental edge. It’s part arcade dogfight, part scientific crusade—proving that even in a neon-green world of fire and steel, every bit of captured data counts.

Every sortie is a test of nerve, precision, and courage. The sky is alive, the stakes are real, and the data you collect could change everything.
